<p><p>The airbrush paint market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based paints contain organic solvents, providing vibrant colors and quick drying times, making them suitable for a variety of surfaces but often emitting stronger fumes and requiring proper ventilation. In contrast, water-based paints use water as a primary thinner, resulting in lower toxicity and easier clean-up, while being more eco-friendly. Both types cater to artists and industries, each offering unique properties to meet diverse application needs.</p></p>
