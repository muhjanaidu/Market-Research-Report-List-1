<p><strong>The Aircraft Machined Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Milled Parts</li><li>Turned Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft machined components market is segmented into milled parts and turned parts. Milled parts are created through a subtractive machining process, where material is removed from a solid block using rotary cutters to produce complex shapes and features, ideal for components like brackets and housings. Turned parts, on the other hand, are produced by rotating the workpiece against a cutting tool, facilitating the production of cylindrical shapes such as shafts and pins, essential for various aircraft assemblies.</p></p>
