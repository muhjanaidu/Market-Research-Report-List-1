<p><p>The ambient lighting market encompasses various types, including recessed lights, surface-mounted lights, suspended lights, track lights, and strip lights. Recessed lights offer a sleek, minimalist aesthetic by fitting into the ceiling, while surface-mounted lights provide flexibility in installation. Suspended lights hang from the ceiling, adding elegance to spaces. Track lights are versatile and allow users to direct light where needed, ideal for galleries or workspaces. Strip lights deliver continuous illumination, perfect for accentuating architectural features or providing task lighting.</p></p>
