<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anticorrosive wood lacquers market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based lacquers. Water-based lacquers utilize water as the primary solvent, offering lower toxicity and a faster drying time, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, solvent-based lacquers contain organic solvents, providing stronger adhesion and enhanced durability against harsh conditions. Each type caters to different user needs, with water-based lacquers preferred for indoor applications and solvent-based lacquers often used in outdoor settings for superior protection.</p></p>
