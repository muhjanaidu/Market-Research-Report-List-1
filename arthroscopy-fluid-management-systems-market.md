<p><p>Arthroscopy fluid management systems are essential in minimally invasive surgeries, providing optimal visualization through effective fluid management. The market is divided into two types: pump management disposables and gravity management disposables. Pump management disposables utilize powered systems to control fluid flow and pressure, ensuring consistent delivery and evacuation of fluids during procedures. In contrast, gravity management disposables rely on gravitational force to manage fluid flow, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for arthroscopic surgeries, though with less precise control.</p></p>
