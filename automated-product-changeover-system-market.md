<p><p>The Automated Product Changeover System market comprises three main types: Fixed Automation, Programmable Automation, and Flexible Automation. Fixed Automation involves dedicated equipment for high-volume production of a single product, optimizing efficiency but lacking adaptability. Programmable Automation allows for reconfiguration to produce different products in medium-volume settings, offering more flexibility. Flexible Automation supports rapid changeover for low-volume, high-variety production, enabling manufacturers to easily adjust to varying demands. Each type addresses specific production needs and operational strategies in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
