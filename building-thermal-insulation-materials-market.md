<p><p>The building thermal insulation materials market encompasses various types, including Stone or Rock Wool, Glass Wool, EPS (Expanded Polystyrene), and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene). Stone or Rock Wool is made from natural minerals, providing excellent fire resistance and sound insulation. Glass Wool consists of fine fibers made from glass, effective for thermal insulation and soundproofing. EPS is a lightweight, cost-effective foam with good thermal properties, while XPS offers high insulation performance with moisture resistance, ideal for construction applications. Other materials may include cellulose and polyurethane, each catering to specific thermal insulation needs.</p></p>
