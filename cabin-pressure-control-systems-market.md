<p><p>Cabin pressure control systems are crucial for maintaining optimal atmospheric conditions in aircraft. The market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic systems autonomously regulate cabin pressure without pilot intervention, ensuring passenger comfort and safety through advanced sensor technology. In contrast, semi-automatic systems require some manual input from pilots to adjust pressure settings, striking a balance between automation and control. Both types enhance operational efficiency and contribute to overall flight safety.</p></p>
