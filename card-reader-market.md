<p><ul><li>Contact Card Reader</li><li>Non-Contact Card Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The card reader market comprises two main types: contact card readers and non-contact card readers. Contact card readers require physical contact with the card, typically utilizing a magnetic stripe or chip to read data during the transaction. In contrast, non-contact card readers enable transactions without direct contact, using technologies like RFID or NFC, allowing users to simply tap or wave their cards for quicker and more convenient payments. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and security needs in modern transactions.</p></p>
