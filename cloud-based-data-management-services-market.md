<p><p>Cloud-based data management services encompass three main market types: Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS), and Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS). SaaS offers ready-to-use applications accessible via the internet, enabling businesses to manage data without extensive IT infrastructure. PaaS provides a platform for developers to build, deploy, and manage applications, facilitating data integration and development. IaaS delivers virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing firms to manage storage, networks, and servers, offering scalability and flexibility for data management needs.</p></p>
