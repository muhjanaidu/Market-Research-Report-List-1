<p><p>The COF (Chip-On-Film) flexible package substrate market is segmented into single-layer and double-layer types. Single-layer substrates feature one conductive layer, simplifying manufacturing and enabling lightweight designs, making them suitable for thin electronic devices. In contrast, double-layer substrates consist of two conductive layers, offering enhanced performance, better thermal management, and greater signal integrity. This dual-layer design is ideal for complex applications requiring higher reliability, such as advanced displays and high-density interconnections in consumer electronics and communication devices.</p></p>
