<p><p>Copper barrier CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential for precision metal removal in semiconductor manufacturing. The market is divided into two main types: non-aqueous copper barrier slurries and aqueous copper barrier slurries. Non-aqueous slurries are solvent-based, providing effective removal of copper while minimizing surface damage, making them suitable for advanced technologies. Aqueous slurries, on the other hand, use water as a primary solvent, improving user safety and environmental compliance, often preferred for mass production processes due to their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
