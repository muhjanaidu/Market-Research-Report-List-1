<p><p>The diesel nozzles market comprises two main types: manual and automatic diesel nozzles. Manual diesel nozzles require human operation, allowing users to control fuel flow, making them suitable for smaller stations or specific applications. In contrast, automatic diesel nozzles feature advanced mechanisms that enable self-shutoff functionality, improving efficiency and safety by preventing overfilling. This automatic type is often preferred in larger fueling operations due to its convenience and reduced labor requirements, catering to the growing demand for efficient fueling solutions.</p></p>
