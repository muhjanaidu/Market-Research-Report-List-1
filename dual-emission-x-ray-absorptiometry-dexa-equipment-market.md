<p><p>The Dual Emission X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment market is divided into two main types: central DEXA bone densitometers and peripheral DEXA bone densitometers. Central DEXA devices are typically used in clinical settings for comprehensive assessment of bone mineral density, providing precise readings for the hip and spine. In contrast, peripheral DEXA devices are more portable and often used in screening processes, evaluating areas like the wrist or heel. Both types serve critical roles in diagnosing and managing osteoporosis and other bone-related conditions.</p></p>
