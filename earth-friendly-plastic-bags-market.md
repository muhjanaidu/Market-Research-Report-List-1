<p><p>Earth-friendly plastic bags encompass various biodegradable materials that minimize environmental impact. Polylactic acid (PLA) bags, made from renewable resources like corn starch, decompose under specific conditions. Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) bags are produced by microbial fermentation and break down in diverse environments. Starch blends combine starch with other biodegradable materials, offering good compostability. The "Others" category includes innovative materials like cellulose and protein-based plastics, contributing to sustainable packaging solutions. Together, these alternatives significantly reduce reliance on conventional plastics.</p></p>
