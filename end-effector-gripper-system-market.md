<p><p>The end-effector gripper system market features various types, each suited for specific applications. Electric grippers offer precise control and integration with robotic arms, making them ideal for delicate tasks. Pneumatic grippers use compressed air for swift actions and high gripping force, suitable for heavy lifting. Vacuum grippers employ suction to handle flat or smooth objects, widely used in packaging and assembly. Magnetic grippers utilize magnets for securely lifting metal items, making them effective in manufacturing and material handling settings.</p></p>
