<p><p>The fast-absorption bioabsorbable stents market comprises two primary types: polymer-based stents and metallic stents. In contrast, metallic stents are constructed from absorbable metals that provide structural support during the healing process before being gradually absorbed. Both types aim to improve vascular healing while minimizing the need for permanent implants, enhancing patient safety and outcomes in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
