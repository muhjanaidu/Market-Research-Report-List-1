<p><p>The fire retardant (FR) foam market is primarily divided into two types: soft foam and hard foam. Soft foam, typically more flexible, is commonly used in applications like upholstery, bedding, and automotive interiors due to its comfort and cushioning properties. Hard foam, on the other hand, is rigid and is often utilized in construction, insulation, and packaging where structural integrity and thermal resistance are essential. Both types are treated with fire-retardant chemicals to enhance safety and reduce fire risks in various environments.</p></p>
