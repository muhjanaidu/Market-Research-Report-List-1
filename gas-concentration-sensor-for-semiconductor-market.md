<p><p>Gas concentration sensors for the semiconductor market are categorized mainly into two types: VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) gas sensors and trace gas sensors. VOC gas sensors detect organic chemicals that can evaporate into the air, crucial for monitoring air quality in industrial and indoor environments. Trace gas sensors, on the other hand, identify low concentrations of gases, often used for environmental monitoring and safety applications. Both types play a vital role in ensuring compliance with safety standards and enhancing operational efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
