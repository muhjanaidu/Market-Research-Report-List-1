<p><ul><li>Thin Film Evaporators</li><li>Short Path Evaporators</li><li>Filter Reactor</li><li>Pressure Reactors & Rotary Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass reactor market includes several specialized equipment types. Thin film evaporators efficiently separate components using a thin film of liquid. Short path evaporators minimize thermal degradation by allowing vapors to travel short distances. Filter reactors enable solid-liquid separation during reactions, enhancing purity. Pressure reactors facilitate high-pressure chemical processes, promoting reaction efficiency. Rotary evaporators are used for solvent removal under reduced pressure, ensuring gentle evaporation. Each type caters to specific applications, driving innovation and demand in the glass reactor industry.</p></p>
