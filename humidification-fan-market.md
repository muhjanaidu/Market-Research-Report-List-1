<p><p>The humidification fan market includes two primary types: centrifugal humidification fans and high-pressure nozzle humidification fans. Centrifugal humidification fans utilize centrifugal force to move air and distribute moisture effectively, making them suitable for larger spaces and industrial applications. In contrast, high-pressure nozzle humidification fans operate by using high-pressure water to create a fine mist, ideal for precise humidity control in smaller areas or specific environments. Both types enhance air quality and comfort in various settings, including industrial, commercial, and residential spaces.</p></p>
