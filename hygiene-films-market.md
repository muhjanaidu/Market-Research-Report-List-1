<p><strong>The Hygiene Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro-Porous Hygiene Films</li><li>Non-Porous Hygiene Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hygiene films market can be categorized into micro-porous and non-porous films. Micro-porous hygiene films feature tiny pores that allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing liquid penetration, making them suitable for breathable applications in products like diapers and feminine hygiene items. In contrast, non-porous hygiene films create a barrier against liquids and contaminants, ensuring complete protection for products such as medical garments and protective covers. Both types serve diverse applications in maintaining hygiene and comfort in various industries.</p></p>
