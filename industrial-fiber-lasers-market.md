<p><strong>The Industrial Fiber Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fiber laser market comprises two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring sustained cutting or welding. They offer high precision and efficiency for materials like metals and polymers. In contrast, Pulsed fiber lasers generate short bursts of energy, suitable for tasks that require intense energy delivery, such as marking, engraving, or micro-machining. Both types find widespread use across various industries.</p></p>
