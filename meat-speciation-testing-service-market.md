<p><p>The Meat Speciation Testing Service market encompasses various methods to identify species in meat products, ensuring food authenticity and safety. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a molecular technique that amplifies specific DNA sequences for accurate species identification. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects proteins related to specific species, offering rapid results. Other methods like LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) utilize advanced analytical techniques for precise detection of species-specific markers. Together, these methods support regulatory compliance and consumer transparency in the meat industry.</p></p>
