<p><p>The medical rehabilitation robotics market is categorized into upper extremity and lower extremity segments. Upper extremity rehabilitation robots focus on aiding recovery for the arms, shoulders, and hands, enhancing mobility and strength after injuries or surgeries. These devices often support tasks like grasping and reaching. In contrast, lower extremity robots target the legs and feet, assisting in gait training and mobility improvement following neurological or orthopedic conditions. Both segments aim to enhance patient outcomes through personalized rehabilitation therapies.</p></p>
