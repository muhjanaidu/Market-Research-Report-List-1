<p><strong>The Medical Tourniquets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Latex Tourniquet</li><li>TPE Tournique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical tourniquet market is primarily divided into two types: latex tourniquets and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditional, elastic devices that provide effective constriction for blood flow control during medical procedures. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some patients. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, are made from synthetic materials, offering improved durability, non-latex options for allergic individuals, and enhanced comfort. Both types cater to specific medical requirements and patient sensitivities in various clinical settings.</p></p>
