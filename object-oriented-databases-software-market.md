<p><p>Object-oriented databases can be deployed in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve hosting the database locally within an organization's infrastructure, offering enhanced control and security but requiring substantial maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based object-oriented databases are hosted on remote servers managed by third-party providers, enabling scalability, flexibility, and reduced upfront costs.
