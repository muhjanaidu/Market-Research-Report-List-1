Halogen lighting offers bright, white light but consumes more power and has a shorter lifespan. HID lighting delivers intense brightness and is effective for long-range visibility, yet can be more expensive and complex to install. Incandescent lighting, while less common due to inefficiency, offers warm light and lower upfront costs.</p></p>
