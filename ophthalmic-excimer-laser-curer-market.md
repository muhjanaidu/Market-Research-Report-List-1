<p><p>The ophthalmic excimer laser market encompasses various laser types used in eye treatments. Diode lasers are compact and efficient, often used in photocoagulation. Femtosecond lasers enable precise corneal procedures with minimal thermal damage. Excimer lasers are pivotal in refractive surgeries like LASIK, reshaping corneas effectively. Nd:YAG lasers are utilized for treating glaucoma and posterior capsule opacification, while Argon lasers are employed for retinal therapies. Lastly, SLT lasers are designed for glaucoma treatment, providing a non-invasive option to lower intraocular pressure.</p></p>
