<p><p>The outdoor pool heat pump market primarily features three types: geothermal heat pumps, air source heat pumps, and water source heat pumps. Geothermal heat pumps leverage stable underground temperatures to provide efficient heating and cooling. Air source heat pumps extract heat from the ambient air, making them versatile and easy to install. Water source heat pumps utilize nearby bodies of water for efficient thermal energy exchange. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to different environmental conditions and user needs in pool heating solutions.</p></p>
