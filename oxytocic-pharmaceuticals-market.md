<p><p>The oxytocic pharmaceuticals market is segmented into two main types: natural oxytocin and synthetic oxytocin derivatives. Natural oxytocin is derived from the pituitary glands of animals and is used primarily to manage labor and postpartum hemorrhage. In contrast, synthetic oxytocin derivatives are chemically synthesized analogs that mimic the effects of natural oxytocin, often used for more specialized medical applications, such as inducing labor or controlling uterine atony. This segmentation caters to diverse therapeutic needs in maternal care.</p></p>
