<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Granule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PA hot melt adhesive market is segmented into two primary types: powder and granule. Powder adhesives consist of fine particles that dissolve when heated, allowing for a controlled application and quick bonding. They are often used in intricate designs or when a smooth finish is essential. Granule adhesives, on the other hand, consist of larger particles that provide a robust bond when melted. They are typically used in bulk applications, offering ease of handling and efficient coverage in industrial processes.</p></p>
