<p><strong>The PEX Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A</li><li>PEX-B</li><li>PEX-C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEX pipe market is categorized into three types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C, distinguished by their manufacturing processes and characteristics. PEX-A pipes, produced through the Engel method, offer superior flexibility and are resistant to freezing. PEX-B, made using a suspension method, is more cost-effective and has good chemical resistance. PEX-C, created via a radiation cross-linking process, has enhanced strength and thermal stability. Each type serves different applications in plumbing, heating, and industrial systems, catering to diverse project needs.</p></p>
