<p><p>The pharmaceutical physical testing market includes various equipment essential for ensuring drug quality and performance. Hardness testers evaluate tablet strength, indicating resistance to mechanical stress. Disintegration testers assess how well tablets break down in a specific solution. Friability testers measure tablet durability under simulated conditions, while tapped density testers determine the compactness of powder formulations. Flowability testers evaluate how easily powders move, which is crucial for consistent manufacturing processes. Together, these testers ensure product reliability and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
