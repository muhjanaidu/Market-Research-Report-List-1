<p><strong>The Picosecond Aesthetic Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nd：YAG</li><li>Nd：YLF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The picosecond aesthetic lasers market includes two primary types: Nd:YAG and Nd:YLF lasers. Nd:YAG lasers utilize neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet for efficient energy absorption, targeting pigment and promoting skin rejuvenation. They are commonly used for tattoo removal, pigmentation treatment, and skin resurfacing. Nd:YLF lasers, on the other hand, employ neodymium-doped yttrium lithium fluoride, offering shorter pulse durations and enhanced precision for delicate procedures. Both technologies contribute significantly to advancements in non-invasive aesthetic treatments, enhancing patient outcomes and safety.</p></p>
