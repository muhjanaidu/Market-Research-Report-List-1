<p><p>The plastic spectacle frame market is categorized into three main types: full rim, half-rim, and rimless frames. Half-rim frames feature a partial rim at the top, offering a lighter appearance while maintaining some structural support. Rimless frames have no visible border around the lenses, delivering a minimalistic aesthetic that prioritizes comfort and a wide field of vision. Each type caters to different style preferences and functional needs.</p></p>
