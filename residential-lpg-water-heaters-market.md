<p><ul><li>Tankless LPG Water Heaters</li><li>Tank LPG Water Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential LPG water heater market consists of two main types: tankless LPG water heaters and tank LPG water heaters. Tankless LPG water heaters provide on-demand hot water, heating it as needed without storing it, making them energy-efficient and space-saving. In contrast, tank LPG water heaters store a specific volume of heated water, providing a readily available supply but occupying more space and potentially using more energy. Each type serves different consumer preferences and household needs in terms of efficiency and capacity.</p></p>
