<p><strong>The Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Tapping Screws</li><li>Non-Self Tapping Screws</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screws market is categorized into self-tapping and non-self-tapping screws. Self-tapping screws are designed to create their own hole in the material, allowing for quick installation without pre-drilling, making them ideal for various applications in metals, plastics, and woods. Non-self-tapping screws, on the other hand, require a pre-drilled hole and are primarily used for more permanent fastening solutions. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics, influencing demand and market growth dynamics.</p></p>
