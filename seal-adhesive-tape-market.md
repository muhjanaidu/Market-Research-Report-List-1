<p><p>The seal adhesive tape market is primarily categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for simple applications like sealing packages or securing items without needing a bonding surface on both sides. Double-sided tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on both sides, allowing for more versatile uses, such as mounting objects or creating seamless bonds between surfaces. Both types cater to various industries, including packaging, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
