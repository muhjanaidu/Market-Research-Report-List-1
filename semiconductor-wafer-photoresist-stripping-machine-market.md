<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Photoresist Stripping Machine market is segmented into two primary types: dry process machines and wet process machines. Dry process machines utilize plasma or vapor technologies to remove photoresist from wafers, offering precise control and minimal chemical use, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, wet process machines employ chemical solutions for photoresist removal, often providing a cost-effective and efficient option for less demanding applications. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
