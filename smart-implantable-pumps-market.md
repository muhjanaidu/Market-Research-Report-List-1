<p><p>The smart implantable pumps market is categorized into micro-pumps and perfusion pumps. Micro-pumps are compact devices designed for precise drug delivery at a microscale, offering enhanced control and minimization of side effects. In contrast, perfusion pumps facilitate continuous or intermittent drug administration to ensure optimal therapeutic levels in tissues, primarily in cancer treatment and pain management. Both types leverage advanced technology for improved patient outcomes, providing personalized treatment options and better management of chronic conditions.</p></p>
