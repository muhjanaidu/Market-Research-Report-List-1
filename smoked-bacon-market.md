<p><ul><li>Hot Smoked Bacon</li><li>Cold Smoked Bacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoked bacon market is primarily divided into two types: hot smoked bacon and cold smoked bacon. Hot smoked bacon is cured and then exposed to smoke at higher temperatures, resulting in a fully cooked product with a rich, smoky flavor, ideal for immediate consumption. In contrast, cold smoked bacon is cured before being smoked at lower temperatures, allowing for a more delicate texture and flavor profile. This type requires further cooking before consumption, appealing to gourmet consumers seeking diverse culinary options.</p></p>
