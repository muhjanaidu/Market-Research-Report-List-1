<p><p>Solar pumps for off-grid irrigation are essential for sustainable agriculture, offering two main types: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater, making them ideal for deep wells, efficiently drawing water without the need for additional infrastructure. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are placed above ground and are suitable for shallow water sources, distributing water through piping systems. Both types harness solar energy, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and providing an eco-friendly solution for irrigation needs.</p></p>
