<p><ul><li>Fusion</li><li>Fixation</li><li>VCF</li><li>Decompression</li><li>Motion Preservation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal implants market encompasses various types that cater to different therapeutic needs. Fusion implants are designed to promote bone growth between vertebrae, stabilizing the spine. Fixation devices, such as screws and rods, secure the spine post-surgery. Vertebral Compression Fracture (VCF) treatments aim to restore vertebral height and alleviate pain. Decompression implants relieve pressure on spinal nerves, while motion preservation technologies maintain natural spinal movement, enhancing functionality and quality of life for patients with spinal disorders.</p></p>
