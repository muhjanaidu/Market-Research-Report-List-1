<p><p>A Staircase Pressurisation System is an essential safety feature in high-rise buildings, designed to maintain a positive pressure in stairwells. The system typically operates by using fans to blow air into the stairwell, creating a pressure differential that keeps smoke at bay.</p><p>The Staircase Pressurisation System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
