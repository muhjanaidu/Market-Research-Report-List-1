<p><ul><li>JCOE Process</li><li>UOE Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The straight seam welded steel pipe market primarily features two production processes: JCOE (J-form, C-form, and O-form) and UOE (U-form and O-form). The JCOE process involves forming steel sheets into a J shape, then a C shape, and finally an O shape, suitable for large diameter pipes. Conversely, the UOE process involves bending steel sheets into a U shape, then an O shape, offering high strength and superior weld integrity. Both processes cater to different applications in industries such as oil and gas, construction, and infrastructure.</p></p>
