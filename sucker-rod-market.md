<p><ul><li>Steel sucker rod</li><li>FRP sucker rod</li><li>Hollow sucker rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sucker rod market can be divided into three main types: steel sucker rods, FRP (fiber-reinforced plastic) sucker rods, and hollow sucker rods. Steel sucker rods are widely used due to their strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. FRP sucker rods offer advantages like corrosion resistance and lighter weight, which enhance longevity and ease of handling. Hollow sucker rods, designed for specific fluid transport tasks, provide additional functionality through their internal space for fluids, improving efficiency in pumping operations.</p></p>
