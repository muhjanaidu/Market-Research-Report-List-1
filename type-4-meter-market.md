<p><p>Type 4 meters encompass two primary market types: electromechanical induction meters and electronic energy meters. Electromechanical induction meters operate using rotating disks and electromagnetic induction, commonly found in older installations. They provide reliable but less precise measurements. In contrast, electronic energy meters utilize digital technology for accurate consumption readings and enhanced features like remote monitoring. This transition reflects an increasing demand for efficiency, data analytics, and modernized infrastructure, catering to both utilities and consumers in the evolving energy landscape.</p></p>
