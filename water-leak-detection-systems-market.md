<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detection Systems</li><li>Active Leak Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water leak detection systems are categorized into passive and active types. Passive leak detection systems monitor areas for potential leaks without actively seeking them, often using materials that can indicate moisture presence. In contrast, active leak detection systems employ sensors and technologies to continuously assess and identify leaks, sending alerts when detected. Active systems provide real-time monitoring and can even shut off water supply to prevent damage, making them essential for safeguarding infrastructures in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
